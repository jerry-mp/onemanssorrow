We Live to Serve...

Man of Sorrows records creates and releases musics across a variety of genres and artists, all with a unified philosophy: to live in the wake of the destruction of the world.

Harsh noise, drone, industrial, black metal--whatever tool is necessary to confront a world that is awash with pain and sorrow. The music of Man of Sorrows records is an exercise in agony and ecstasy; a catharsis.
